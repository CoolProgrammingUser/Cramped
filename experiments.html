Conversations

For the longest time, I had been suspecting that I was talking to more girls than boys. I would always try to treat everyone equally, but I couldn't shake the feeling that things weren't quite equal. I thought it could be all in my head, but then again, maybe it wasn't, and if so, was it caused by me or other people? After driving myself crazy going back and forth forever, I decided to finally get scientific about it and collect some data pertaining to female versus male interactions (excluding adults). The results are detailed below.
